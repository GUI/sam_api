Field reference for HMDA LAR data

Lenders covered under HMDA are required to keep a Loan Application Register (LAR). Each time someone applies for a loan at a HMDA-covered institution, that lender is required to make a corresponding entry into the LAR. A LAR for a given calendar year contains a record of all applications that reached a final action in that year (regardless of what year it was submitted). The type of information reported in the LAR is listed in this table.
